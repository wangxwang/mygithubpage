About

Currently completing a B.A. Computer Science with a Studio Art minor

I am intrigued by the intersection of art and technology; in more specific terms, I am interested in making both art and technology accessible to people from all walks of life, especially those who are underpriviliged and underserved.

I believe that taking in interdisciplinary approach to both Computer Science and Art, I am allowed to tackle all problems both in between and beyond. I believe I can do so in a manner that addresses all of the intricacies of a problem not otherwise seen.

In my free time, I enjoy bad jokes and clicking on too many related videos on Youtube.
